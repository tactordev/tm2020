# Trackmania 2020
A game full of wonder. Where the community builds maps, hosts competitions and excels. 


I am building mods for this game, finally! :D 

This is the first mod, Map Battles (suggested by Jnic, check out his twitch!). Want to 1v1 others on maps without the stress of elo in ranked? Want to choose who you play against for some fun? That's what it's for.
